Maid of Honour

Sanober has been my heterosexual life partner since we were forced to live together back in 2005 when we were both rejected from UVic housing. Bonding over pop-punk and perogies, we ended up being able to live together for 6 years...and I only threw pizza at her once! Our friendship has brought us through university, kareoke bars, and different cities, but she’ll be standing beside me like she has been for the last 10 years.

Bridesmaid

Cindy is my aunt, my sister, my best friend. From dying my hair red-orange and teaching me how to dance to “Gettin Jiggy With It”, to life lessons about why it’s important to wear deodorant, I am where I am today (and on my wedding day) because of the light encouragement she gave Amesh to finally ask me to marry him. She will stand beside me on one of the most important days of my life, because I don’t remember a day when she hasn’t been beside me.

Laurel Temmel

Many brunches, gin and tonics, americanos, hanging in the great outdoors, and debates about the merits of Dungeons & Dragons later, Laurel is still the one to roll her eyes at me when I’m being obnoxious.

A shuttle bus will pick up guests from their accommodations and bring them to the venue, as well as back to their accommodations after the reception.

We will have more information on busses closer to the date of the wedding.


If you wish to drive to the venue there is parking at the farm but no overnight parking.
