The Wedding

We’re so excited you’ll be with us to celebrate!

The Ceremony

The ceremony will be held at 3:30pm in Beacon Hill Park, Victoria close to the intersection of Oliphant Ave. and Heywood Ave. Limited parking is available by the play ground and cricket field, so we encourage folks to walk or taxi if possible. Keep an eye out for decorations and signs in the park with directions to the ceremony.

We’re crossing our fingers for a lovely summer day, but also suggest keeping an umbrella handy as the ceremony will move forward with light rain.

The ceremony will be short and sweet, and with limited seating, we ask that priority be given to family and folks who need it - Thanks!

The Reception

Dancing? Yes please!

The reception will begin at 7:00pm at 1701 Douglas St. (Victoria Public Market at the Hudson). This will be a stand-up cocktail event with seating available. Gourmet street food is on the menu with plenty allergy friendly, vegetarian, and gluten-free options. Let us know of any additional dietary choices/restrictions by email at rudiferwedding@gmail.com.

There will also be a toonie bar serving up Hoyne craft beer, wine, and cider. Please remember to send us your two songs for dancing if you haven’t already! google maps link
